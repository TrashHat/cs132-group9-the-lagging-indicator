SDG 8 · Decent Work & Economic Growth
Crash Course: A Sectoral Analysis of Philippine Stock Exchange Performance During and After COVID-19 (2019–2025)

This project examines whether the COVID-19 pandemic affected all sectors of the Philippine stock market equally, and whether the subsequent recovery has been truly broad-based or concentrated in select sectors. Using daily closing price data from all six PSE sector indices spanning 2019 to 2025, this analysis investigates the crash and recovery arc of the Philippine economy through the lens of SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth.
