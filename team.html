About This Project

The Lagging Indicator is a CS 132 capstone project submitted as part of the PilipiNuts 2026 initiative, a class-wide effort to apply data science to Philippine datasets in the context of the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

This project focuses on SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth, examining whether the Philippine Stock Exchange's post-COVID recovery was sector-inclusive or concentrated in a few areas. The name is a reference to the lagging indicator concept in economics, particularly in trading, wherein the indicators used for stock market trading often reflects economic conditions with a delay. This project investigates the duration of that lag for each sector following the 2020 crash.
